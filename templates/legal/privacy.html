MatchForge is built privacy-first. This policy explains what we collect, where it lives, and your rights.

1. Our Privacy Principles

  • Your data is yours — we do not sell personal information
  • Local-first when self-hosted — you control your server
  • Minimal collection — only what the toolbox needs
  • Transparent AI processing — you consent before image analysis
  • Deletion rights — you can remove your data

2. What We Store

DataPurpose
Email addressAccount sign-in and verification
Profile settingsGoals, optional bio, handle, avatar/selfie
Preference vectorPersonalized ranking weights
Screenshots you uploadVision extraction, trust scoring, ranking
Analysis resultsScores, trust badges, explanations
Policy acceptanceTimestamp and version you agreed to

3. Where Data Lives

Self-hosted: Data stays in your PostgreSQL database and storage on your server.

Hosted: Data is stored in your account on our infrastructure. When AI is enabled, screenshots and text may be sent to configured providers (e.g. xAI Grok) for analysis. We do not use your data to train third-party models.

4. AI Processing and Consent

Uploading screenshots, avatars, selfies, or examples means you consent to automated analysis for extraction, trust scoring, and ranking.

5. Social Enrichment

May retrieve publicly available information only — not private accounts or DMs.

6. What We Do Not Do

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share screenshots with other users (unless you use share)
  • Publish ranked lists of real people

7. Your Rights (Canada / PIPEDA)

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Contact the operator for hosted deployments; self-hosted operators handle requests directly.

8. Retention & Security

Data is retained while your account is active. Delete profile workups from the dashboard or request account deletion. We use HTTPS and access controls; protect your credentials.

9. Children

MatchForge is for adults (18+).
