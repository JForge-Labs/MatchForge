What MatchForge does

MatchForge is a personal safety and basic due diligence tool. You upload screenshots of publicly visible dating profiles — the same photos, bios, and prompts any user on that platform could normally see. Our AI helps you spot authenticity concerns, compatibility signals, and basic safety red flags before you invest more time or agree to meet someone.

  • Public sources only — we do not access private databases, hidden accounts, or non-public records.
  • Your private toolbox — analysis stays in your account unless you choose to share.
  • AI is imperfect — scores are estimates, not facts. False positives and false negatives happen.
  • You stay responsible — verify important details yourself; MatchForge is not a background check.
  • Delete when done — remove profile workups from your dashboard anytime after review.

To use MatchForge you must read and accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. By continuing, you consent to AI processing of images you upload and confirm you will only submit publicly visible or lawfully shared content for your own personal safety decisions.
