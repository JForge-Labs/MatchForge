1. What MatchForge Is — Personal Safety & Due Diligence

MatchForge is a personal safety and basic due diligence tool for adults making informed decisions about people they may meet through dating or social platforms.

You upload screenshots of publicly visible dating profiles — the same information any user with an account on that platform could ordinarily see. MatchForge performs AI-assisted analysis for authenticity, compatibility, and safety signals.

MatchForge is decision-support software — not a dating service, licensed background-check provider, or source of ground truth. We do not access private databases or assume non-public information.
